What is the Bit Cruiser?

The Bit Cruiser is a synthesizer plugin developed Tom Couto and Joe White. The Bit Cruiser has built-in filter, crusher, and reverb effects.

As of today, the plugin is compatible with Ableton Live, Garageband, Logic Pro, and LMMS.
